Welcome to Off The Grid Living!

This lifestyle is all about being independent. People who are living off the grid constantly rely on solar energy. This energy helps with growing food, and also helps disconnect from day-to-day lifestyle of today. This lifestyle is simple and can be fun for those who want to disconnect from the technology of the world.

Whether you are looking to go completely off the grid or just want to relax/disconnect from technology, this is the lifestyle for you!

Beginners Tips

  • Use any form of Solar Power
  • Collect rainwater in empty containers
  • Composting and Recyle for Sustainability
  • Grow simple crops like potatoes and tomatoes
